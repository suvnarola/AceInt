Introduction

This tutorial will guide you thorught the process of setting up your email client to recieve your Empire Trade emails. To do this you need 3 things; your email username, password and your SMTP server address. To get your username and password just call the IT department of the Empire Trade Head office and they will provide you with it. To get your SMTP email address, contact your ISP or network administrator.

Outlook Express 6 Instructions

Starting the mail account wizard

Firstly you need to open Outlook Express. Then click the "Tools" menu, located at the top of the window. Click "Accounts" in the menu that drops down.

In the window that comes up, click the "Add" button, then select "Mail..." from the pop out menu.

Account Wizard - Step 1

The account wizard window should now be on your screen. The first thing that it will want to know is your name, type your name in the text field provided. The name that you type in here will be sent with any email that you send. Once you have entered your name, click the next button.

Account Wizard - Step 2

This step, Outlook wants to know the email address that you will be using, if you do not know this, call head office on (+61 7 5437 7220). Enter your full email address in the box that is required (eg. john.d@au.empirexchange.com). Once you have done this press the next button.

Account Wizard - Step 3

From the drop-down box that specifies the type of incoming mail server, make sure that POP3 is selected. In the incoming mail server, enter "mail-01.server-logon.com" this will tell your computer to connect to the Empire Trade email server. Enter your network email server in the Outgoing mail server field. If you do not know the name of this server, contact your network administrator or internet service provider. Once you have entered this information, click the "Next" button

Account Wizard - Step 4

Enter your username and password in the fields that are provided. These are specifically for your email only and you will need to get this from Empire Trade Head office if you don't already have it. Be sure to check the "Remember Password" box.  Leave the "Secure Password Authentication" checkbox unticked. Once this information has been entered, click the "Next" button.

Account Wizard - Step 5

This step is just a page informing you that the process of adding an account is now finished. Click the Finish button to continue

Account Selection Screen

This will bring you back to the account selection screen. Select the account that you just created then click the "Set as Default" button to the right. Then click the "Close" button to complete the process.

Outlook 2002 Instructions

Starting the mail account wizard

To begin this tutorial you must have your mail inbox open in Microsoft Outlook. On the menu bar at the top of the window, click "Tools", this will cause a mreny to drop down. From that menu, select "E-mail Accounts...". This will begin the Account wizard

Account Wizzard - Step 1

With the account wizzard open on your screen, place a dot next to the "Add a new e-mail account" option by click in the circle to the left of it. Click the next button.

Account Wizzard - Step 2

The next step is to select the type of account that you want to add. E Banc Trade emails are dispursed using a POP3 server. Place a dot next to "POP3". Press the "Next" button to continue the tutorial.

Account Wizard - Step 3

There is lots of information for you to enter on this step. First put your name in the field provided. Enter the email address that you are setting up for in the box below. In the incoming mail server field put "mail-01.server-logon.com" this is the server that your Empire Trade email is retrieved from. In the box below enter your local networks email server, if you do not know what this is contact your network administrator or internet service provider. Enter your email username and password into the fields provided. Note this is different to your Administration logon. Be sure to tick the "Remember password" box. If you need any of this information other than your local network email server you can contact Empire Trade head office. Once this information has been entered, click the "Next" button.

Account Wizard - Step 4

This step is meerly a notice informing you that your email has been set-up. Click the Finish button to complete the process.
