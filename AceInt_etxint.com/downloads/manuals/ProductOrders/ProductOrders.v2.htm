E Banc Trade Product Ordering System

Introduction

The product ordering system was designed to assist E Banc Trade Licensees in obtaining E Banc Trade products and promotional literature. This second installment of the system was developed to fix the many problems that arose from the first system. This tutorial file was written to assist Licensees and Staff that are using the system. To navigate the different parts of this tutorial file, simply click the links in the bar above. If you would like to print this file out, click the Print icon at the top left of this page.

Main Menu

The main menu is always present at the top of the product orders system, this is designed to be an easy way for you to navigate between past and present orders. There are three buttons in the main menu; "View Past Orders", "Current Order" and "User Manual". The "View Past Orders" button will bring up the past orders screen, for more information on this screen visit the Past Orders section of the manual. The "Current Order" button will bring up your current unsent order. The "User Manual" button will bring up this manual, do not hesitate to use this if you are ever stuck.

The order panel

The order panel (on the right) displays the order that you are currently modifying in summation form. To the left is a sample order panel, move your mouse over the different parts of the panel for a description.

The panel is titled according to the order that you are modifying. For the example that we have, this is our current unsent order, and is thus titled "Current Order". If we were modifying an order that we had sent, the order would be titled "Order #[num]".

Under the title, the name of the person that sent through the order is displayed. Eg. "Josiah Truasheim" is ordering this order.

By clicking the print icon, you can print out your current order.

The select box below this is where you can select where you would like the order to be delivered to. To change this, simply click the down button to the right, than scroll to the area that you would like this delivered to (Areas are listed alphabetically).

The products that you have ordered are displayed below the area select box. Each product will display the quantity that you have ordered (in bold), the name of the product, and the amount that you will be charged for that product. To make modifications to the quantity of a product, click the "Change" link. This will bring you the item's category, so that you can modify it. To remove a product from your order, click the "Remove" link.

The total cost of the order is displayed after all the products.

The status of your comments is displayed below the products and total. When the status says "No Comments", this means that you have not added comments to your order. If the status says "Comments Added" than this indicates that you have added comments to your order. Below this is a link that will let you add/change the comments attached to the order.

The "Clear this order [X]" link will remove the order that you are working on from the system. This is permanent, you will not be able to retrieve this order.

The "Send this order [>>]" link will send your unsent order to Head Office for processing. This link will not appear if you are modifying an order that you have sent. This is because the order does not need to be sent again, Head Office will recieve any changes that you make.

The Products Panel

The products panel (located on the left) holds all the products that are available. The category select, located at the top of this panel, allows you to jump between the different categories. To change your category, simply click the down button on the right, then scroll to the category that you wish to edit.

There are two "special" categories in the category select; "Business Cards" and "Comments". For more information on these categories, select the Business Cards & Comments Section of this manual

In a normal category, a product boxes like the ones to the right will appear.

If the category that you are viewing contains any products that you have ordered (for example our plastic bags) they will appear in a blue box with the quantity that you have selected.

Products that are available, that you have not ordered will display in a grey box.

Warning
If you enter a quantity for a product, and do not click the "Change Order" / "Add to Order" link the order will NOT be added. You MUST click the "Change Order" / "Add to Order" link for EVERY product that you would like to add to your order

At the top of the product box there are two things, on the left there is the product name, on the right is the "Change Order" / "Add to Order" link. When you click this link, the quantity of the products that you have selected will be added / changed in your order.

The bottom of the product orders box contains 3 more things: The price of the products per unit, the quantity entry box and the calculated total for the quantity of products that you require.

Comments

The comments section is where you can add comments to your order, so that you can provide additional instructions with your order. To add or change the comments for your order, simply type your comments in the box provieded, than click the "Add Comments" / "Change Comments" link in the bottom right.

Business Cards

The business card selection system can be acessed by selecting "Business Cards" from the category select box. There is four parts to ordering a business card.

The area selection box to the left is where you select the area that you would like the cards from. Areas are listed alphabetically.

When you have selected an area, the Staff member selection box will display the staff from the area that you selected. Select the staff member that you wish to order cards for from this box.

When you have selected a staff member you will be able to select a quantity from the Quantity selection box. Select the quantity that you want from this box.

If you wish to add comments specifically relating to the business card that you have selected, enter them in the box provieded.

When you have entered the correct informtion, click the "Add to Order" button to add this business card to your order.

Once the business card has been added to your order it will be visible in the order panel. It will appear simmilar to the other products in the panel, bar one thing, instead of a "Change" link, there is a "View" link so that you can view a sample of the card that you have ordered.

Past Orders

The past orders menu can be accessed by selecting the "View Past Orders" link on the main menu. This will bring up a list of all the orders that are available. You can use the controls in the menu to choose what information you would like displayed. If the show order contents box is ticked, the items in the order will be displayed in addition to the title and area. The "show comments for order" box defines if the comments for a the orders are to be displayed. The other two check boxes will define what type of orders are to be displayed. You must select one or both of these to have ane orders displayed. Only orders from the areas that you have access to are displayed.
